About us

　TV TOKYO was founded in 1964. Our main news program "World Business Satellite"is the longest regular news in Japan and has won the most prestagious TV prize many times. In fact, the numbers of our viewers is more than 10 million people.
　We are specialised in ecnomics and politics as a group of NIKKEI and Finacial times. Also we are digging into people and social problems deeper.

　London Bureau are covering all of EMEA area from London. We have covered over 30 countries including Africa, Middle East and East Europe.
　We are always looking for some interesting stories. If you know stories or are interested in our job.Feel free to Contact us through the form below.
